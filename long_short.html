Seizure Connectivity Patterns

Visualizing state/parameter estimation of a neural mass model

Background

We track the states and parameters of a neural mass model during recordings of epilepic seizures. The model has three neural populations (ensembles): Excitatory, Inhibitory & Pyramidal.

(Fast) states represent the activation of each neural ensemble. (Slowly varying) parameters represent connectivity between neural ensembles.
