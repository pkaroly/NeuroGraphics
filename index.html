About

This website is devoted to the epilepsy research and neuroengineering work done at the Cook Lab (Department of Medicine & St Vincent's Hospital Melbourne, The University of Melbourne). The purpose of this website is to present our research graphically.

Our primary goal is to use computational, mathematical and engineering methods to describe brain dynamics, with a particular focus on understanding and preventing epileptic seizures.
